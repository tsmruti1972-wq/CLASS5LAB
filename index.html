Frequently Asked Questions

Find your advisor in the student portal under "Advising"; you can also contact your department's advising office via the Registrar's site to schedule an appointment.
Registration windows depend on class standing and program. Use the student portal registration page and consult your advisor before enrolling. Check the academic calendar for key dates.
Canvas is SMU's learning management system. Log in with your SMU credentials at the Canvas portal to access syllabi, assignments, and course announcements.
Move-in dates are assigned by residence hall. Bring photo ID, housing paperwork, bedding, and personal items. Check your residence life email for exact times and check-in locations.
Meal plans are managed through Dining Services. You can view dining locations, hours, and plan balances on the Dining page; most plans include access to campus dining halls and flex dollars.
Student Health & Counseling offers medical and mental health services. Visit the Student Health page to see hours, how to book appointments, and walk-in availability.
Call 911 for emergencies. For campus security or non-emergency assistance, contact SMU Police (listed on the Campus Safety page) — they operate 24/7.
Tuition billing and payment information are available in the student portal under Billing/Finance. You can set up payment plans and view due dates there.
Contact the SMU IT Help Desk for account issues, email setup, and software access. Their support pages include phone, chat, and knowledge base resources.
Visit the Student Life or Campus Activities pages to browse student organizations, upcoming events, and the involvement fair schedule. Joining clubs is a great way to meet peers.
