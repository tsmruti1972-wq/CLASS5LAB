Orientation & First Weeks

Attend your assigned orientation sessions, check the official orientation schedule on the SMU site, and complete any required online modules before arrival.

Typical steps:

  1. Confirm move-in date and on-campus check-in
  2. Complete online orientation tasks
  3. Meet your academic advisor and review your degree plan
  4. Attend Welcome Week events and student organization fair

Academics & Advising

Find your advisor via the Registrar or your academic department pages. Review degree requirements, set up Canvas and campus email, and register for required first-semester courses early.

Housing & Dining

Review residence life policies, move-in checklists, and meal plan options on the Housing and Dining pages. Bring required documentation for check-in and follow move-in schedules.

Health, Safety & Support

SMU provides student health services and counseling; review hours and appointment procedures on the Student Health page. For emergencies, follow campus safety instructions or contact emergency services.

Campus Life

Explore clubs, student organizations, intramurals, and volunteer opportunities. The Student Life pages list ways to get involved and upcoming events.
